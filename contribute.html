Want to contribute?

There are many ways to contribute to Hypatia's continued development.

Develop Hypatia

Hypatia is open source software, meaning you can help it's development. Hop on over to our GitHub repository, and get stuck in!

Fund our development

Hypatia is developed by volunteers from around the globe. You can help fund it's development through either donating via PayPal or contributing to the author's Patreon.

Spread the word!

The more people using Hypatia, both developers and users alike, the better it will get. Share Hypatia on your social networks, and get the word out!
