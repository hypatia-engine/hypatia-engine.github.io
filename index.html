Hypatia is a game engine for single player 2D action adventure games, like The Legend Of Zelda: Oracle of Ages and Oracle of Seasons. Hypatia is aimed at non-programmers and programmers alike.

Sound interesting? Find out how to contribute, or get started with Hypatia, without any programming knowledge.
